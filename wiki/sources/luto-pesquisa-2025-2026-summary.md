# Luto e Perda: Pesquisa 2025-2026

## Resumo

Esta pesquisa-compilada sintetiza as evidências científicas mais recentes sobre o uso de ayahuasca no tratamento de luto complicado, com foco no ensaio clínico randomizado publicado no Nature Scientific Reports (2025) que validou o modelo A-MR (Ayahuasca + Meaning Reconstruction).

## Contexto Clínico

### Luto Complicado (Prolonged Grief Disorder)

O luto complicado affects approximately 6-7% of bereaved individuals and is characterized by:

- Symptoms persisting beyond 12 months
- Inability to accept the reality of loss
- Excessive rumination and avoidance
- Significant impairment in daily functioning

### Limitações das Abordagens Tradicionais

```
ABORDAGENS TRADICIONAIS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Terapia cognitivo-comportamental:limited efficacy for meaning-making
• Psicodinâmica: lengthy process, variable outcomes
• Farmacoterapia: targets symptoms, not underlying mechanisms

• RESULTADO: Many individuals do not respond adequately
```

## Meaning Reconstruction Therapy (MRT)

### Origens e Fundamentos

Desenvolvida por Robert Neimeyer, MRT treats grief as a "meaning crisis":

```
CORE PRINCIPLES:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Grief as crisis of meaning, not just "process"
2. Disruption of "global assumptions" about world
3. Need to reconstruct narrative post-loss
4. Search for "meaning ads" in suffering
```

### Os Três Movimentos

```
MRT THREE MOVEMENTS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MOVIMENTO 1: SENSE-MAKING
Question: "Why did this happen?"
Goal: Process the narrative of loss

MOVIMENTO 2: BENEFIT-FINDING
Question: "What can I learn from this?"
Goal: Identify post-traumatic growth

MOVIMENTO 3: IDENTITY RECONSTRUCTION
Question: "Who am I now?"
Goal: Reformulate sense of self
```

## Implicações Clínicas

### Quando Considerar Ayahuasca + MRT

```
INDICAÇÕES:
━━━━━━━━━━

• Prolonged Grief Disorder (>12 months)
• Inadequate response to traditional therapy
• Prior positive experience with psychedelics
• Motivation for deep inner work
• Access to qualified therapeutic support
```

### Contraindicações

```
CONTRAINDICAÇÕES:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Psychosis or bipolar disorder
• Active suicidal ideation
• Certain medications (MAOIs, SSRIs)
• History of adverse psychedelic reactions
• Unprepared individuals without support
```

## Research Gaps e Futuras Direções

### O Que Ainda Não Sabemos

1. **Long-term effects** beyond 3 months
2. **Optimal dosing** and number of sessions
3. **Mechanisms** of unity experiences with deceased
4. **Comparison** with psilocybin + MRT
5. **Individual differences** in response
