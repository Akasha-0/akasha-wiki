# Ayahuasca vs Psilocybin vs MDMA for Trauma: Research Summary

## Resumo

Comparação entre ayahuasca, psilocybina e MDMA para tratamento de PTSD e trauma. Cada composto tem indicações preferenciais baseadas em mecanismos distintos, com ayahuasca demonstrando superioridade para processamento de trauma específico.

## Key Findings

### Mechanisms Comparison

| Compound | Primary Mechanism | PTSD Efficacy | Duration |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|----------|
| Ayahuasca | DMT + MAOi (5-HT2A) | **Superior** | 6-8h |
| Psilocybin | 5-HT2A | Moderate | 4-6h |
| MDMA | Serotonin + Oxytocin | Superior (Fase 3) | 4-6h |

### Clinical Evidence

**Veteran Study (July 2025)**
- Direct comparison: ayahuasca vs. psilocybin retreats
- Ayahuasca: greater PCL-5 reduction
- Both: improved sleep and quality of life
- Ayahuasca superior for fear processing

**MAPS MDMA Phase 3**
- 67% remission in PTSD
- FDA Breakthrough Therapy designation
- 3 sessions with prepared therapy

## Compound-Specific Indications

### Ayahuasca: When Preferred
- PTSD with specific traumatic memories
- Deep trauma processing
- Ceremonial context desired
- Somatic processing (purge) beneficial

### Psilocybin: When Preferred
- Depression as primary concern
- Patient prefers less intense experience
- Structured clinical setting
- Philosophical/spiritual insights sought

### MDMA: When Preferred
- PTSD with trust/vulnerability issues
- Abuse trauma with shame/isolation
- Need for emotional support during memory work
- Social anxiety significant

## Safety Profile

| Risk | Ayahuasca | Psilocybin | MDMA |
|------|-----------|------------|------|
| Serotonin syndrome (with SSRIs) | **High** (MAOi) | Low | Low |
| Psychosis | Risk in predisposed | Risk in predisposed | Low |
| Dependency | Low | Low | Moderate |

## Clinical Takeaway

```
AYAHUASCA: Best for trauma-focused PTSD treatment
PSILOCYBINA: Best for depression and meaning-making
MDMA: Best for trauma with trust issues (when FDA-approved)
```
