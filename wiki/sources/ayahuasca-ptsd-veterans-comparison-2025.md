# Ayahuasca vs Psilocibina: PTSD Veteran Comparison Study (2025)

## Overview

A direct comparison study between ayahuasca and psilocybin retreats for military veterans with PTSD. This is one of the first head-to-head comparisons of psychedelic compounds for trauma treatment.

## Study Design

```
DESIGN: Direct comparison between psilocybin and ayahuasca retreats
POPULATION: Military veterans with PTSD
MEASURE: PCL-5 (official PTSD scale)
OUTCOMES: Sleep quality, quality of life, symptom reduction
```

## Key Results

### Primary Outcome: PCL-5 Reduction

| Compound | PCL-5 Reduction |
|----------|-----------------|
| Ayahuasca | **Greater reduction** |
| Psilocybin | Significant but lesser |

### Secondary Outcomes

- Both groups: Significant improvements in sleep
- Both groups: Significant improvements in quality of life
- Ayahuasca group: Better processing of fear and trauma specifically

## Why Ayahuasca Performed Better for PTSD

### Mechanistic Explanation

```
TRAUMA → Amígdala hiperativa → Flashbacks, evitação
         ↓
Ayahuasca mechanisms:
1. Reduces ego filter (DMN)
2. Desensitizes amygdala gradually
3. Allows memory "reconsolidation" with new meaning
4. Increases connectivity with prefrontal cortex (control)
```

### Compound-Specific Advantages

| Aspect | Ayahuasca | Psilocybin |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Mechanism for fear | Superior | Moderate |
| Trauma processing | Deeper access | Good |
| Depression | Effective | Effective |
| Addictions | Moderate | Superior |
| Duration of mystical experience | Longer | Shorter |

## Interpretation

Ayahuasca appears particularly suited for PTSD because:

1. **Longer duration** (6-8 hours) allows deeper processing of trauma
2. **MAOi component** (harmalas) may enhance DMT's effect on fear circuits
3. **Traditional ritual context** provides psychological safety for trauma work
4. **Nature of experience** tends to be more confronting, allowing direct trauma processing
