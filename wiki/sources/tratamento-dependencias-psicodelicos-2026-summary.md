# Addiction Treatment with Psychedelics 2025-2026

## Summary

Synthesizes 2025-2026 research on psychedelic-assisted addiction treatment. Classic psychedelics (ayahuasca, psilocybin, LSD) and related compounds (5-MeO-DMT, ibogaine) demonstrate therapeutic potential across addiction types.

## Key Findings

### By Substance
- **Ayahuasca**: AUD reduction in traditional settings; cocaine reinstatement prevention (mice, 2025)
- **Psilocybin**: Significant smoking cessation; fewer heavy drinking days for AUD
- **5-MeO-DMT**: 66% alcoholism improvement; p=0.03 mystical experience correlation
- **Ibogaine**: Withdrawal/craving reduction; cardiac safety concerns

### Koob-Volkow Framework
Three disrupted systems addressed by psychedelics:
1. Incentive salience (reward circuits)
2. Negative affect (emotional pain)
3. Executive function (inhibitory control)
