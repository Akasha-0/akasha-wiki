# Psilocybin Clinical Trials 2024-2026 Summary

## Overview

The period 2024-2026 represents a watershed moment for psilocybin clinical research, with the completion of the largest publicly funded trial to date and ambitious plans for the largest psychedelic trial ever conducted.

## Major Clinical Trials

### EPISODE Trial (Phase 2b) - Completed

**Lead Institutions**: Central Institute for Mental Health (ZI) Mannheim and Charité Berlin

**Details**:
- 144 patients with treatment-resistant unipolar depression
- Three-arm design: high dose, low dose, or control substance
- Largest publicly funded psilocybin clinical trial to date

**Key Publication**:
- Mertens et al.: "Efficacy and safety of psilocybin in treatment-resistant major depression: the EPISODE randomized clinical trial" - JAMA Psychiatry

**Significance**: Demonstrated significant efficacy for treatment-resistant depression with favorable safety profile.

### DiMension Trial (Phase 3) - Planned 2025/2026

**Lead Institution**: European consortium

**Details**:
- Over 700 patients planned
- Psilocybin plus psychotherapy over 32 weeks
- Goal: evaluate psilocybin efficacy compared to standard antidepressant treatment
- Aim: bring psilocybin into public reimbursement systems

**Significance**: Will be the largest psychedelic trial ever conducted, potentially paving way for regulatory approval and widespread access.
