# DiMension Phase 3 Trial: Largest Psilocybin Study Planned

## Overview

The DiMension trial is an upcoming Phase 3 clinical trial planned for 2025-2026 that will be the largest psychedelic clinical trial ever conducted, with over 700 patients planned.

## Trial Design

**Location**: Europe

**Participants**: 700+ patients

**Treatment**: Psilocybin plus psychotherapy

**Duration**: 32 weeks

**Primary Goal**: Evaluate psilocybin efficacy compared to standard antidepressant treatment

**Key Objective**: Bring psilocybin into public reimbursement systems (health insurance coverage)

## Significance

### Scale
- Largest psychedelic trial ever conducted
- 5x larger than typical Phase 2b trials
- Designed for regulatory and reimbursement impact

### Strategic Importance
- Aims to demonstrate cost-effectiveness for health systems
- Could enable prescription coverage under public health insurance
- Pivotal for European regulatory approval pathway
