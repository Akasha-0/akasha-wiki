# EPISODE Trial: Phase 2b Psilocybin for Treatment-Resistant Depression

## Overview

The EPISODE (Efficacy and safety of Psilocybin in treatment-resistant dEpression) trial was a landmark Phase 2b randomized clinical trial investigating psilocybin for treatment-resistant major depression. It represents the largest publicly funded psilocybin clinical trial to date.

## Trial Design

**Institutions**: Central Institute for Mental Health (ZI) Mannheim and Charité Berlin

**Participants**: 144 patients with treatment-resistant unipolar depression

**Design**: Three-arm randomized controlled trial
- High dose psilocybin
- Low dose psilocybin
- Control substance (placebo comparison)

## Key Results

**Publication**: Mertens et al., JAMA Psychiatry (2025)

**Findings**:
- Significant reduction in treatment-resistant depression symptoms
- Favorable safety profile
- Dose-dependent effects observed

## Significance

1. **Largest publicly funded trial**: Demonstrates academic/government interest beyond commercial sponsors
2. **Independent replication**: Validates findings from Compass Pathways commercial trials
3. **Regulatory pathway**: Data supports potential future approval applications
