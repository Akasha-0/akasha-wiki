# Johannes T. Reckweg

## Overview

Johannes T. Reckweg is a clinical researcher who has conducted Phase 1/2 trials on 5-MeO-DMT for treatment-resistant depression and safety studies. His work establishes the clinical safety profile of 5-MeO-DMT.

## Key Research Contributions

### 1. Phase 1/2 Trial for Treatment-Resistant Depression
- Study of vaporized 5-MeO-DMT formulation (GH001)
- Patients with treatment-resistant depression
- Safety and efficacy assessment

### 2. Safety and Dosing Studies
- Phase 1 dose-ranging study in healthy volunteers
- Doses: 2, 6, 12, 18 mg
- Showed dose-dependent subjective effects
- Generally safe and well-tolerated

### 3. Clinical Pharmacology Review
- Comprehensive review: "The clinical pharmacology and potential therapeutic applications of 5-methoxy-N,N-dimethyltryptamine (5-MeO-DMT)"
- Co-authored with Uthaug, Szabo, Riba, Ramaekers
- Essential reference for 5-MeO-DMT clinical applications

2. Reckweg JT, van Leeuwen CJ, Henquet C, et al. A phase 1/2 trial to assess safety and efficacy of a vaporized 5-MeO-DMT formulation (GH001) in patients with treatment-resistant depression. Front Psych. 2023. PMC10319409

3. Reckweg J, Mason NL, van Leeuwen C, et al. A phase 1, dose-ranging study to assess safety and psychoactive effects of a vaporized 5-MeO-DMT formulation (GH001) in healthy volunteers. Front Pharmacol. 2021. PMC8667866
