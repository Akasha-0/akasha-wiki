# Synthesis Institute

## Overview

Based in Amsterdam, Netherlands, the Synthesis Institute is a leading European training provider for psychedelic facilitation. The institute focuses on training professional facilitators for psychedelic-assisted therapy, with an emphasis on both personal development and clinical skills.

## Program: Psychedelic Practitioner Core Training

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Duration** | 13 months |
| **Focus** | Professional facilitator training |
| **Method** | Combination of theory and experiential practice |

## Philosophy

Synthesis emphasizes:

- Professional development of psychedelic facilitators
- Integration of personal experience with therapeutic skills
- Evidence-based approaches combined with contemplative traditions
- Ethical practice and harm reduction

## Key Features

- Comprehensive facilitator training
- Personal experiential components
- Focus on set and setting principles
- Integration of somatic and psychotherapeutic approaches
- Emphasis on ethical considerations and legal compliance

## Program Strengths

- European perspective on psychedelic therapy
- Located in Amsterdam - a city with relatively progressive drug policies
- Focus on professionalization of the field
- Connection to broader psychedelic research community
