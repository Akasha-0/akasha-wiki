# OVID Clinic Berlin

## Overview

OVID Clinic Berlin is a research clinic conducting clinical trials on psychedelic-assisted therapy, including studies on 5-MeO-DMT for depression and LSD derivatives for generalized anxiety disorder.

## Key Research

### Active Studies

**5-MeO-DMT for Depression**
- Investigating 5-MeO-DMT as treatment for depression
- Approved by BfArM (German Federal Institute for Drugs and Medical Devices)
- Ethics committee approved

**LSD Derivative for Generalized Anxiety Disorder**
- Novel LSD-based compound for anxiety treatment
- Regulatory and ethics approvals obtained

## Context

OVID Clinic represents the expanding European clinical trial infrastructure for psychedelic medicine, with Germany becoming a hub for Phase 1/2 trials due to favorable regulatory environment (BfArM approval process).
