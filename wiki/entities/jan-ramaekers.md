# Jan G. Ramaemakers

## Overview

Jan G. Ramaekers is a psychopharmacology researcher who has conducted critical clinical trials establishing the safety and efficacy of 5-MeO-DMT for depression, anxiety, and related conditions.

## Key Research Contributions

### 1. Clinical Pharmacology of 5-MeO-DMT
- Comprehensive review of 5-MeO-DMT clinical applications
- Established safety profile for human use
- Dosing guidelines

### 2. Phase 1 Safety Trials
- Dose-ranging studies in healthy volunteers
- Phase 1/2 trials for treatment-resistant depression
- Showed adverse events are generally transient

### 3. Anti-inflammatory Effects
- Study showing 5-MeO-DMT modulates innate immune response
- Sigma-1 receptor involvement
- IL-6 reduction pathway
