# Cottonwood Tucson

## Summary

Cottonwood Tucson is a residential addiction treatment program in Tucson, Arizona that integrates ayahuasca ceremonies into a comprehensive 9-month treatment protocol. The program combines traditional therapeutic approaches with plant medicine ceremonies to address substance use disorders and underlying trauma.

## Program Components

- **Duration:** 9 months residential
- **Ceremonies:** Regular ayahuasca ceremonies
- **Psychotherapy:** Individual therapy sessions
- **Community Living:** Shared residential environment
- **Support Groups:** Peer support groups
- **Physical Activities:** Recreation and exercise
- **Spiritual Development:** Personal growth and spiritual exploration

## Treatment Focus

- **Trauma Processing:** Addressing underlying traumatic experiences that fuel addiction
- **Identity Reconstruction:** Redefining sense of self beyond addiction
- **Support Network Building:** Developing sober community connections
- **Coping Skills:** Building sustainable emotional regulation tools

## Key Features

The program recognizes that addiction often masks deeper emotional pain, and ayahuasca ceremonies are used to access and process these underlying issues. The residential format allows for sustained integration work following the psychedelic experiences.
