# Natalie M. Uthaug

## Overview

Natalie M. Uthaug has conducted extensive research on 5-MeO-DMT, particularly its effects on depression, anxiety, and mindfulness-related capacities. Her work includes both clinical and naturalistic studies.

## Key Research Areas

### 1. Single-Inhalation Study (n=42)
- First study to examine vaporized 5-MeO-DMT in naturalistic setting
- Sustained enhancement of satisfaction with life
- Increased mindfulness-related capacities
- Decreased psychopathological symptoms

### 2. Prospective Examination: IL-6 and Cortisol
- Study of anti-inflammatory effects (IL-6 decrease)
- Effects on cortisol levels
- Non-judgment aspect of mindfulness

### 3. Reactivation Patterns
- Research on reactivation experiences after 5-MeO-DMT
- Comparison of vaporization vs intramuscular injection
