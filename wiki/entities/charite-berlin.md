# Charité Berlin

## Overview

Charité Berlin is one of Europe's largest university hospitals and a leading medical research institution. It collaborated with the Central Institute for Mental Health (ZI) Mannheim on the landmark EPISODE Phase 2b trial for psilocybin treatment of treatment-resistant depression.

## Key Research

### EPISODE Trial (Phase 2b)

- **Role**: Collaborating institution with ZI Mannheim
- **Focus**: Treatment-resistant unipolar depression
- **Significance**: Largest publicly funded psilocybin clinical trial

**Key Publication**:
- Mertens et al. "Efficacy and safety of psilocybin in treatment-resistant major depression: the EPISODE randomized clinical trial" - JAMA Psychiatry (2025)
