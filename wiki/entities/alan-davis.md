# Alan K. Davis

## Overview

Alan K. Davis is a researcher specializing in 5-MeO-DMT research, trauma treatment with psychedelics, and naturalistic psychedelic experiences. His work focuses on real-world outcomes from psychedelic use outside clinical settings.

## Key Research Areas

### 1. 5-MeO-DMT Epidemiology and Outcomes
- **Large Survey Study (n=515)**: Most comprehensive study of 5-MeO-DMT users
  - 66% reported improvements in alcoholism
  - Benefits for depression, anxiety, PTSD, SUD
- **Naturalistic Setting Research**: Effects in non-clinical environments

### 2. Trauma Treatment with Special Operations Veterans
- Prospective examination of psychedelic treatment for co-occurring alcohol misuse and PTSD
- Showed improvements in psychological and cognitive impairment
- Special operations forces veterans population

### 3. Psychedelic Reactivations
- Research on "flashbacks" after 5-MeO-DMT (27-73% experience them)
- Generally positive or neutral experiences
- Predictors of reactivation experiences
