# Tropical Liss

## Summary

Tropical Liss is a Thailand-based addiction treatment program that combines ayahuasca ceremonies with traditional Thai healing practices including Vipassana meditation, yoga, traditional massage, and dietary protocols. The program offers a shorter-term intensive format compared to residential programs.

## Treatment Components

- **Ayahuasca Ceremonies:** 3-4 sessions
- **Vipassana Meditation:** Traditional Buddhist insight meditation practice
- **Yoga:** Mind-body integration practices
- **Traditional Thai Massage:** Bodywork for nervous system regulation
- **Diet:** Clean dietary protocols supporting the healing process

## Approach

The program takes an integrated approach, combining the transformative potential of ayahuasca with established contemplative and somatic practices. This multi-modal format supports both the psychedelic experience and its integration.
