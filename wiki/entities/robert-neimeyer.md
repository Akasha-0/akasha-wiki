# Robert Neimeyer

## Resumo

Robert Neimeyer is a pioneering psychologist and the developer of Meaning Reconstruction Therapy (MRT), a therapeutic approach for grief that treats bereavement as a crisis of meaning rather than merely a process of stages. His work forms the therapeutic foundation of the A-MR (Ayahuasca + Meaning Reconstruction) model used in the 2025 Nature Scientific Reports clinical trial.

## Sobre

- **Área:** Psicologia Clinica, Tanatologia
- **Contribuição Principal:** Teoria e terapia de Reconstrução de Significado para luto
- **Influência:** Fundamenta a integração psicoterapêutica no modelo A-MR

## Meaning Reconstruction Therapy (MRT)

### Fundamentos Teóricos

Neimeyer's MRT is built on the premise that grief is fundamentally a "crisis of meaning." When we lose someone:

- Our "world of meaning" is disrupted
- Beliefs about the world, self, and others are challenged
- We must reconstruct a new meaning system to continue living

### Os Três Movimentos da MRT

```
MOVIMENTO 1: SENSE-MAKING (Dar Sentido)
"Por que isso aconteceu?"
- Processar a narrativa do evento
- Compreender a perda e seu significado

MOVIMENTO 2: BENEFIT-FINDING (Encontrar Benefício)
"O que posso aprender com isso?"
- Identificar crescimento a partir da perda
- Transformar dor em propósito

MOVIMENTO 3: IDENTITY-RECONSTRUCTION (Reconstruir Identidade)
"Quem eu sou agora?"
- Reformular o senso de self
- Integrar a perda na narrativa de vida
```

## Relevance to A-MR Model

The 2025 Nature Scientific Reports trial combined MRT with ayahuasca (A-MR model):

- MRT provides the therapeutic framework for meaning reconstruction
- Ayahuasca enhances the process by reducing ego defense, increasing neuroplasticity, and producing unity experiences
- The combination showed superior outcomes compared to MRT alone
