# CIIS - California Institute of Integral Studies

## Overview

CIIS is a leading institution in psychedelic therapy training, offering one of the most comprehensive programs in the United States. Located in San Francisco, California, the program draws on decades of psychedelic research and integrates multiple therapeutic modalities.

## Program Details

### Psychedelic Therapy Training Program

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Duration** | 10 months |
| **Total Hours** | 140-150 hours curricular |
| **Additional Work** | ~120 hours reading preparation |
| **Format** | In-person lectures, small group discussions |
| **Location** | San Francisco, CA |

## Curriculum Components

- Lectures and small group discussions
- Analysis of recorded therapy sessions
- Role-play and experiential practices
- Guided imagery exercises
- Expressive arts and writing projects
- Final project work (~18-20 hours)

## Substances Covered

The program provides training across multiple psychedelic compounds:

- MDMA
- Psilocybin
- Ketamine
- Ayahuasca (brief overview)
- Ibogaine (brief overview)
- LSD and cannabis

## Prerequisites

- Advanced degree OR
- Bachelor's degree (common for career changers)

## Cost Structure

Program costs typically range between **$5,000-15,000** depending on specific track and materials.

## Key Strengths

- Established reputation in the field
- Integration of multiple therapeutic traditions
- Experienced faculty with clinical expertise
- Focus on both theoretical knowledge and experiential learning
- Preparation for clinical practice
