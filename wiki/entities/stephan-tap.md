# Stephan C Tap

## Overview

Stephan C. Tap is a researcher who authored the seminal 2024 paper exploring 5-MeO-DMT as a potential treatment for alcohol use disorder (AUD). This paper represents one of the first comprehensive reviews of 5-MeO-DMT's therapeutic mechanisms for addiction.

## Key Publication

**Tap SC. (2024)**  
*The potential of 5-methoxy-N,N-dimethyltryptamine in the treatment of alcohol use disorder: A first look at therapeutic mechanisms of action*  
Addiction Biology. 29(4):e13386  
PMCID: PMC11007263

## Contribution

Tap's paper provides a systematic review of 5-MeO-DMT literature, examining:
1. Mystical experience as mediator of therapeutic outcomes
2. Ego-dissolution mechanisms
3. Mindfulness and psychological flexibility
4. Neuroimaging findings (EEG, SPECT)
5. Preclinical neurobiological mechanisms

## Key Hypotheses

Tap proposes that 5-MeO-DMT treats AUD by:
- Alleviating psychiatric mood-related comorbidities (depression, anxiety, PTSD)
- Challenging rigid negative self-perceptions through ego-dissolution
- Improving psychological flexibility to reduce experiential avoidance
- Modulating neural oscillations for improved executive control
