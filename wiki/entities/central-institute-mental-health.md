# Central Institute for Mental Health (ZI Mannheim)

## Overview

The Central Institute for Mental Health (Zentralinstitut für Seelische Gesundheit, ZI) in Mannheim, Germany is a leading psychiatric research institution that conducted the landmark EPISODE Phase 2b trial for psilocybin treatment of treatment-resistant depression.

## Key Research

### EPISODE Trial (Phase 2b)

- **Role**: Lead institution in collaboration with Charité Berlin
- **Design**: Randomized clinical trial with 144 patients
- **Condition**: Treatment-resistant unipolar depression
- **Arms**: High dose psilocybin, low dose psilocybin, control substance
- **Significance**: Largest publicly funded psilocybin clinical trial to date

**Key Publication**:
- Mertens et al. "Efficacy and safety of psilocybin in treatment-resistant major depression: the EPISODE randomized clinical trial" - JAMA Psychiatry (2025)
