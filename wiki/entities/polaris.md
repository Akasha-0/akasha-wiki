# Polaris (Canada)

## Overview

Polaris is a Canadian-based psychedelic therapy training program that specializes in plant medicine integration and harm reduction. The program is notable for its focus on Indigenous wisdom and Western therapeutic frameworks.

## Focus Areas

- Plant medicine integration
- Harm reduction approaches
- Integration of Indigenous and Western knowledge systems
- Trauma-informed care
- Ceremony and traditional practices

## Key Characteristics

Polaris distinguishes itself through:

- Deep engagement with Indigenous traditions
- Emphasis on harm reduction frameworks
- Focus on integration practices
- Connection to Canadian regulatory landscape
- Preparation for emerging legal contexts

## Unique Position

Canada has taken a relatively progressive stance on psychedelic medicines compared to many other countries. Polaris trains practitioners to work within this evolving legal framework while respecting traditional plant medicine practices.

## Training Philosophy

The program emphasizes:

- Reciprocity with plant teachers
- Respect for Indigenous knowledge
- Integration of personal practice with professional service
- Trauma-informed approaches
- Community-centered care
