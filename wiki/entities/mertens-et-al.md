# Gerhard Mertens

## Overview

Gerhard Mertens is a researcher affiliated with the Central Institute for Mental Health (ZI) Mannheim and Charité Berlin who led the landmark EPISODE Phase 2b trial investigating psilocybin for treatment-resistant depression.

## Key Publication

**Mertens et al. (2025)**
- Title: "Efficacy and safety of psilocybin in treatment-resistant major depression: the EPISODE randomized clinical trial"
- Journal: JAMA Psychiatry
- Role: Lead author

## Research Focus

- Psilocybin clinical trials for depression
- Treatment-resistant depression interventions
- Clinical trial methodology
